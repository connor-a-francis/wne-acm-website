About us

This is the homepage for ACM at WNE. We are a group of like-minded people with a shared interest in computing, and with a shared goal of joining the workforce prepared and excited for the future! We meet on Wednesdays at 5pm, in Herman Hall, Room 207 (The CSIT lounge). In the spirit of a computing club, this website is open source, so feel free to inspect/submit changes to it at the GitHub Repo. (Even if you don't know what you're doing!)

Resume Night

Stop by for our collaboration with representatives from the Career Center and get personalized and actionable resume advice to help break into the workforce!
